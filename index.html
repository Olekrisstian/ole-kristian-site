Ole Kristian Einarsen

Music Therapist, musician.

Ole Kristian Einarsen is a trained music therapist and musician from the Grieg Academy. He is currently working on his PhD project Music, Health and Policy. His research explores how child-centred, music therapy-informed health research can support and ensure cultural participation for displaced children who have fled alone across international borders. Ole Kristian has toured and performed on bass and violin, contributing to several recordings with various bands in Norway and across Europe, including Great News, Charlie Skien and Forza.
