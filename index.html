Ole Kristian Einarsen

Music Therapist, musician.

Ole Kristian Einarsen is a trained music therapist from the Grieg Academy currently working on his PhD project "Music, Health and Policy". In his research, he is investigating how child-focused and music therapy-based health research on how to provide and secure cultural participation for young people fleeing alone over international borders.
